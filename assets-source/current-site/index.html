Our History

Our History

Started in 1994 by Nistor & Floare Forgaciu, The Good Samaritan was founded to provide food, aid and support to disadvantaged families in Romania. Today, their dream continues as we broaden our vision to reach and support families and communities worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help and serve widows, orphans, families and local church communities by the grace of God with a humble and obedient heart.
