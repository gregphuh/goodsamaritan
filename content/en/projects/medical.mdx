**Marta Lutac** is in cancer treatment. Her grandson **Bogdan** needed two eye surgeries this year. The board visited the Lutac family in Rauseni, Botosani county, in summer 2024. We prayed with them and covered medicine and hospital travel for both Marta and Bogdan.

The **Maicaru family** is also walking through cancer. Sister Ileana was diagnosed early in the year. We helped with medication costs and the long drives to specialty hospitals in larger Romanian cities.

Romania's public health system covers basic care, but families in rural regions face transport costs and medication gaps that quickly add up. A single trip to a regional hospital from Botosani can cost a family a week of groceries.

## How medical aid works

Pastors notify us when a family is in crisis. We respond within the same week. Funds are wired through trusted in-country partners who know the families and have walked with us for years. Every medical disbursement is logged in the 2024 annual report.
