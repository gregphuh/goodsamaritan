Romanian winters reach minus twenty Celsius in the worst weeks. Families without firewood or paid utility bills face real risk.

In 2024 the **Ciobanasu family** in Avrameni received firewood, a winter supply of food, and help with their utility bills. The **Murariu family** in Moldova received a full firewood delivery in late autumn, before the snow.

## How winter aid works

The board pre-orders firewood in early autumn from a Romanian supplier we have worked with for years. We deliver to families on a list compiled by local pastors in October. Bills are paid directly to utility companies; no cash changes hands.

Average winter aid for one family runs between $50 and $200 depending on home size, family composition, and the year's heating costs.

## Give to winter aid

The Firewood and winter fund is replenished each year between September and November. Monthly gifts are the most useful pattern.
