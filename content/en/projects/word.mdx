Mission support is the oldest line in our work. From the year we were founded, we have prioritized standing alongside the people who live where the Gospel needs to be carried — village pastors in rural Romania, church planters in Moldova, missionary families in the Middle East and Africa, and partner ministries in the US and the UK.

We do this in two ways. First, direct support: monthly stipends and one-time grants sent to named missionaries the board knows personally, in amounts that meaningfully cover rent, transport, materials, and family expenses. Second, partnership: grants to established mission organizations like APME and YWAM who carry the work into places we cannot reach directly.

## How mission support works

A board member or trusted local pastor brings the request. The board reviews the worker's call, their accountability structure, and the field need. Support is approved as a recurring stipend or a one-time grant, sent directly to the worker via Zelle, bank transfer, or — for international fields — through a partner organization with established remittance channels.

## Give to the Word of God fund

A monthly gift to the Mission fund means the board can say yes when the next worker in the next field writes asking for help with school fees for their kids, a vehicle repair, or a one-month rent gap.
