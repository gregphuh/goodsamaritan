In September 2023 the **Dragomir family** received the keys to a home in Otelu Rosu, purchased by Good Samaritan after a year of saving. We visited them in 2024 and they have settled in.

The **Anton family** in Comana de Sus reached us in late 2024 with a different need. Their home was unheated and the walls were uninsulated. Winter was coming. The board funded insulation for one wall and helped them weatherize the windows.

The **Barbalata family** had a home but no electricity. In 2024 we paid the connection cost so they could light the rooms after sundown and warm them with electric heat when the firewood ran low.

Each home is named, photographed in the 2024 annual report, and visited by a board member in person.

## How housing aid works

We do not buy houses speculatively. Each project starts with a request from a local pastor who knows the family. The board reviews, visits, prays, and commits.

Average cost for a home repair in rural Romania is between $250 and $2,000. Larger purchases happen when the board has saved enough and the family's situation requires it.
